Our project was deployed to observe any significant data coming from longitude of cities and major weather effects. We look at temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed in our analysis. The analysis included data gathered on the same date in over 500 cities on 01/05/2017.

The data was cleaned through Pandas, while the use of MatPlotLib was employed to build the scatter plots below:

  • City Latitude vs. Max Temperature
  • City Latitude vs. Humidity
  • City Latitude vs. Cloudiness
  • City Latitude vs. Windspeed
